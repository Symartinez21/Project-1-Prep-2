Meet The Artist

Hello! I’m Sophia Martinez and I am a proud Filipino-American raised and based in sunny California’s Bay Area. My art is inspired by how video games intersect and evoke the vibrance of childhood memories. Show and tell in elementary school built the foundation of my ethos as an emerging artist. The way that video games make me feel… I wish to share that with the world through digital art. Whether you are looking for animations, 3D modeling, or a creative hand in coding- I am equipped to work with whimsy and fun!
